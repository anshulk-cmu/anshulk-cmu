## 🔬 Research Interests

I work on mechanistic interpretability, with one question driving most of it: why do capable models fail to use knowledge they clearly encode? Two of my current projects hit the same gap from different angles. In LLM world-model evaluation, linear probes recover property-valuation signal at 90 to 96% accuracy, yet steering on that signal barely moves predictions. In arithmetic reasoning, linear subspaces turn out to be necessary but not sufficient for how the model composes multiplication. Seven years shipping AI products before research is why I care where these failures land in deployment, not only in the activations.
